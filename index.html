About Bobby

Bobby is a mixed media artist originally from eastern Iowa. He co-owned a bar with his brother where Bobby designed all of the weekly advertisements by hand. Bob has been creating art since 1985 and obtained a degree from the Institute of Commercial art in Omaha, Nebraska. His art comes in many mediums, but primarily he paints with oil and acrylic (and anything else he finds interests him!). Bobby has mainly creating sports themed art, but has branched into religious motifs as well. Enjoy the gallery below from Bobby's instagram and if you are interested in purchasing any of Bobby's art, or commissioning a piece, please reach out either by instagram direct message, facebook message or email, all located in the contact section!
